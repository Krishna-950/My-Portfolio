SPEC-03 / BUILD LOG

PROJECT WORK

8 UNITS DOCUMENTED
STATUS: ALL OPERATIONAL
UNIT-01PLC · SCADA · PNEUMATICS

Automated Material Classification System

A conveyor-fed sorting machine with three selectable modules — metal, color, and volume — run from a single HMI panel. Inductive, TCS230 color, and optical sensors feed a Siemens S7-1200 PLC programmed in TIA Portal V17.1, which fires pneumatic double-acting cylinders to route each object into its basket.

UNIT-02PLC · HYDRAULICS · RECYCLING

Modular Plastic Moulding System

A cyclic moulding rig for recycled thermoplastics: an induction-heated chamber melts material for gravity-fed discharge into travelling mould boxes, where a PLC-synchronized hydraulic press compresses the cooling semi-solid charge — running fill, cool, and press stages in parallel without pre-pelletization.

UNIT-03IoT · ESP32-CAM · OCR

Number Plate Recognition & Register System

A push-button ESP32-CAM captures a plate image, runs it through cloud OCR, and logs the result to Firebase in real time — shown on an OLED and a live website. Field-tested on five institute buses for fully automated entry/exit register-keeping.

UNIT-04IoT · RASPBERRY PI PICO W

Smart Fluid Analyzer for Industries

An end-to-end fluid diagnostics platform reading temperature, TDS, turbidity, gas signature, color, UV-fluorescence, and pH, then classifying the sample into one of twenty categories on a live dashboard. Hit 100% classification accuracy across 8 fluids at under ₹3,000 in parts and zero hosting cost.

UNIT-05IoT · ESP32-CAM · BIOMETRICS

Smart Hostel Mess Entrance Monitor

A face-recognition entry system for hostel mess halls using ESP32-CAM and InsightFace cosine-similarity matching, with tiered daily-entry limits for staff, residents, and day scholars and automatic buzzer alerts on membership expiry — fully containerized on a cloud backend.

UNIT-06ARDUINO · SOLIDWORKS

Smart Object Sorting System

A low-cost Arduino Uno sorter combining IR, inductive, raindrop, and TCS230 color sensors with stepper and servo motors to classify objects by material and color — prototyped first in SolidWorks, with HMI/SCADA added via TIA Portal V17 for industrial-grade communication.

UNIT-07HFSS · 5G · ML

Dual-Band 5G mmWave Antenna (n260/n261)

A compact circular microstrip patch antenna on Rogers RO3003 substrate covering the 28/38 GHz bands, achieving 6.38/5.8 dB gain and 80–88% radiation efficiency, with ML-assisted optimization of return loss and bandwidth in HFSS.

UNIT-08HFSS · 5G · RF DESIGN

5G mmWave Antenna — n258 Band

A slotted microstrip patch antenna at 25.5 GHz on Rogers RT5880 substrate, with slot-loading to widen bandwidth and stabilize radiation pattern — simulated to a 7.4 dBi peak gain, >85% efficiency, and below −18 dB reflection coefficient.
